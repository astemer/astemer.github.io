How to use the map when Offline?

Chrome:
This browser do not allow to work in “offline” mode, but it does not get to this state if you do not close the browser.
Best: Leave your browser open, and use other tabs. Do not close the chrome app.
To save the page for future use: Open the browser side menu. Press on the "Dowload" sign (Arrow pointing down).
To open the page in the future: Open the browser side menu. Press on "Downloads". Look for your saved page title - Click it.

iPhone Safari:
This browser do not allow to work in “offline” mode, but does not get to this state if you do not close the browser
Best: Leave your browser open, and use other tabs. Do not close the Safari app.
To save the page for future use: Click the “Export” icon on the bottom of the browser (A box with arrow pointing up).
Then, click “Add to reading list” or “Add to bookmark” title.
To open the page in the future: Click on the “Reading list” icon on the bottom of the browser (looks like a book).
Then select the page you saved.
